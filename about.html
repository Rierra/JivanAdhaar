About Jeevan Adhaar

Serving communities, transforming lives since 2018

Our Story

Jeevan Adhaar Foundation was established in 2018 by a group of young professionals and social workers who witnessed the stark disparity between urban and rural India. What started as weekend health camps in villages around Lucknow has grown into a multi-state operation impacting over 50,000 lives annually.

Our founders, led by Dr. Rajesh Sharma and Smt. Priya Mishra, left their corporate careers to dedicate themselves full-time to community development. Today, Jeevan Adhaar operates across 8 states with a team of 45 full-time staff and over 500 committed volunteers.

We believe that sustainable change happens when communities are empowered to lead their own development. That's why we focus on capacity building, skill development, and creating local leadership rather than just providing aid.

Our Vision

A just and equitable India where every individual has access to quality healthcare, education, and opportunities for dignified livelihoods, regardless of their socio-economic background or geographical location.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities through integrated development programs in healthcare, education, livelihoods, and environment, while fostering local leadership and sustainable solutions.
