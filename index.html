Welcome to Jivan Adhaar

Jivan Adhaar is a registered non-governmental organization committed to improving the quality of life for marginalized communities across India. Established in 2018, we work at the grassroots level to address critical challenges in healthcare, education, and livelihood.

Our programs reach over 50,000 beneficiaries annually across 8 states, with a focus on sustainable development, community participation, and partnerships with government bodies.
