Our Programs

Integrated development initiatives for lasting community impact

Rural Healthcare

Our flagship program addresses the critical gap in healthcare access for rural communities. We operate mobile medical units, conduct specialized health camps, and run awareness programs on preventive healthcare.

Key Initiatives:

  • Monthly health camps covering 8-10 villages each
  • Free eye screening and cataract surgery referrals
  • Diabetes and hypertension screening with follow-up
  • Maternal and child health programs
  • Telemedicine consultations with specialists

Education Support

Education is the cornerstone of sustainable development. Our education program focuses on ensuring quality learning opportunities for underprivileged children while supporting government schools with infrastructure and resources.

Key Initiatives:

  • Scholarships for meritorious students (Rs. 12,000/year)
  • Remedial classes for slow learners
  • Computer labs in 15 government schools
  • Library and reading room setups
  • Career counseling for Class 10-12 students

Women Empowerment

Empowering women is central to community development. Our program helps women achieve economic independence through skill training, self-help groups, and microfinance support while addressing issues of health and social awareness.

Key Initiatives:

  • 42 active Self-Help Groups (SHGs)
  • Vocational training: tailoring, beauty, food processing
  • Microfinance linkage with nationalized banks
  • Legal awareness and rights education
  • Menstrual hygiene awareness programs

Environment Conservation

Environmental sustainability is integral to rural development. Our green initiatives focus on increasing forest cover, promoting water conservation, and encouraging sustainable agricultural practices among farming communities.

Key Initiatives:

  • Annual tree plantation drives (Van Mahotsav)
  • Rainwater harvesting system installations
  • Check dam construction for water conservation
  • Organic farming training and certification support
  • Plastic-free village campaigns

Youth Development

India's demographic dividend can only be realized through meaningful engagement of young people. Our youth program focuses on building leadership, promoting community service, and preparing youth for employment and entrepreneurship.

Key Initiatives:

  • Youth Leadership Camps (7-day residential)
  • Career guidance and soft skills training
  • Digital literacy and computer courses
  • Volunteer mobilization for community projects
  • Sports and cultural activities promotion

Disaster Relief

When calamity strikes, Jivan Adhaar mobilizes rapidly to provide emergency relief and long-term rehabilitation support. Our trained volunteers and established community networks enable quick response even in remote areas.

Key Initiatives:

  • Emergency food and medicine distribution
  • Temporary shelter and sanitation facilities
  • Coordination with district administration
  • Post-disaster livelihood restoration
  • Community preparedness training
